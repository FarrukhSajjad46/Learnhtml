HTMl Centered

To center text and images, we can use CSS styling.

The style="text-align:center" attribute can be used to center text and images.

Example

    <div style="text-align: center">
     <h1> I am a centered heading. </h1>
     <p> I am a centered paragraph. </p>
     <img src="images/example.jpg" />
    </div>

info Note!   This method can NOT center Block-level elements. You will learn about block-level elements later.
